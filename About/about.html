I am a Scientist,

widely acclaimed as the father of the internet in Africa

I earned a PhD in Computer Science in 1977, from the State University of New York at Stony Brook and helped establish the Computer Science and Information Technology Department at the University of Cape Coast in Ghana, where I taught since 1979.

I then assisted in implementing the transformational new technology throughout sub-Saharan Africa.
